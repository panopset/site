flexible

Panopset desktop JavaFX applications are open source. You have as many ways to install and use them, as is possible with the latest Java releases. Download a pre-built installer, or build your own installer. Download the panopset.jar all-in one jar for your platform, or build your own. Download the source and use it in your own maven or gradle project, it doesn't matter. Whatever way to launch, that best suits your needs, is the right way.
